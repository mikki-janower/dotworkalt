Mikki Janower  dot  Work Armour Showreel Index

Commissions, collaborations, and fever dreams compiled over the course of four years at Armour.

Armour is Washington University's style & culture publication. As Editor-in-Chief, led a full visual rebrand; comissioned photographs, illustrations, and prose; and oversaw collaborations with Unbound and Pebble, among others.

Art Director 2017-2018; Editor-in-Chief 2018-2020

A million thanks to the entire Armour staff.
