Mikki Janower  dot  Work Nike: Discover Your Air Index

Discover Your Air is a '90s-infused campaign promoting a series of sneaker drops for Nike and Foot Locker. As a creative intern at Bullish, Inc., I worked on art direction, graphic design, and copywriting for deliverables including a game show hosted by iconic rapper Fat Joe and time capsule "unearthings" by NBA star Jayson Tatum and rapper Playboi Carti.

Our campaign also featured pop-up events and influencer features across the country and a series of vertical iPhone videos by Rick Jacques.

Mikki Janower is a graphic designer and art director based in Brooklyn. Her work reflects her multidisciplinary background, robust visual research, and acute typophilia. She loves freelance projects and collaborations, particularly work skewed towards music and the arts.

She recently recieved a BFA in Communication Design from Washington University in St. Louis and is currently looking for postgraduate opportunities. Previous clients include Nike and The Governor's Ball Music Festival; previous internships include The Collected Works.
